## English

We plan to use two LP451165 cells (3.7 V, 300 mAh, nominal 4.5 x 11 x 65 mm) as a matched 1S2P battery pack. Please provide traceable documents showing supplier name, part number, revision and date:

1. Formal Cell Specification and Pack Specification PDFs.
2. Complete mechanical drawing including tolerances, pouch seals, tabs, wires and PCM.
3. Weight, maximum thickness, initial thickness and swelling after cycling.
4. Rated/minimum capacity and test conditions.
5. Standard and maximum charge current and termination current.
6. Recommended, maximum continuous and pulse discharge current, including pulse duration and cooling conditions.
7. 0.2C, 0.5C, 1C and 2C discharge curves at 25°C.
8. Discharge curves at 0°C, 25°C and 45°C.
9. Initial/maximum DC internal resistance and DCIR after 100/300/500 cycles.
10. Cycle-life and capacity-retention data.
11. Safety test reports for overcharge, over-discharge, short circuit, crush, drop and temperature.
12. UN38.3, IEC 62133, UL1642 if available, MSDS and shipping documents.
13. 10 kΩ NTC option, beta value, tolerance and placement.
14. 1S2P matching limits for open-circuit voltage, capacity, DCIR, lot and aging time.
15. Per-cell fuse/PTC/fusible-link options.
16. 1S2P pack continuous/pulse current ratings and protection thresholds.
17. MOQ, sample lead time, production lead time and lifecycle commitment.

Please quote 10 standard cells, 10 high-rate cells and 5 supplier-matched/protected 1S2P packs.
